Features of Wild-X.

Premium Design
Wild-X boasts a premium design that seamlessly merges style and functionality, setting a new standard for electric adventure motorcycles.
Swappable Battery System
Wild-X introduces a convenient swappable battery system, enabling riders to effortlessly replace batteries for extended journeys and uninterrupted adventures.
150Nm Torque
Wild X's impressive 150Nm torque delivers exhilarating acceleration, ensuring thrilling rides and unmatched performance for adventure enthusiasts.
Wild-X's Ergonomics
Wild X prioritizes rider comfort with its ergonomic design, offering a tailored riding experience that enhances control and adventure.
Shock-Proof
Wild X's shockproof feature ensures durability on rugged terrains, providing riders a smooth and secure adventure experience in any condition.
200km Range
Empowered by a robust 45Ah battery pack, Wild X offers an impressive 200km range, granting riders extended journeys without compromise.
